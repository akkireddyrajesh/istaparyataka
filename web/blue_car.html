Below Features are our specialities

Planning of trips is now hassle free and manage according to your destinations where we offer multiple serene packages, which makes one of the best caravan to travel with us.

Economical Package (Rs 35/Km)

In this pack we charge according to kilometres you travelled, we provide cleaner, driver and fuel where the customer need to bear all the remaining expenses which include state tax, tollgate charge and parking charges.

Bussiness Package (Rs 55/Km)

This is similar to economical pack, as usual, we charge according to kilometres you travelled, we provide cleaner, driver, fuel and bear all the expenses which include state taxes, tollgate charges and parking charges.

Deluxe Package (Rs 10,000/day)

This pack is irrespective of kilometres you travel as we charge according to the number of days you owed the caravan, we provide cleaner, driver, full tank fuel and bear all the expenses which include state taxes, tollgate charges and parking charges except fuel charges which need to be borne by customer.

Note :

Driver and cleaner are provided only if a customer needed (Business and Deluxe pack)

The vehicle should be returned as given, including full tank diesel (Deluxe pack) and without damage if cleaner and driver are not provided by us.
